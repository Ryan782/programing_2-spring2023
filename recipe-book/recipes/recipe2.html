Ryans Signature Bean Egg Pizza

Description

This is a much tastier version of pizza. I like to call this dish the new and improved pizza, if you thought pinapple on your pizza was good you will have your socks blown off by the egg bean combo.

Ingredients

  • One Pre-Rolled Pizza Dough
  • 3 Cans of Bushes Baked Beans Ranch style
  • 4 Eggs (Must Be From 7 Year Old Chicken)

Steps

  1. Start by pre heating your oven to 310 degrese Kelvin
  2. Next take your pizza dough and baked beans and spread the ranch style baked beans all over the pizza dough, make sure there is an even coat
  3. Next put the dough in the oven and cook for 2 minutes
  4. Final step is to remove pizza let rest for 19 minutes and then add 4 uncooked eggs and enjoy
