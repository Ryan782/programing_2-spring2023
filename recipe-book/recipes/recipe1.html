Ryans Signature Peanut Butter Sandwich

Description

This is a much tastier version of a peanut butter sandwich and will bring you back to your childhood. The sandwich is made with White Bread, Peanut Butter, and most importantly 13 green olives.

Ingredients

  • Two Slices Of DJUNGELBREAD White Bread
  • 14 fl ounces of Crunchy Peanut Butter
  • 13 Green Olives

Steps

  1. Start by taking two slices of white bread and voraciously beat them with a rolling pin (this makes the bread cooperate).
  2. Next take your can of peanut butter out of the freezer and lather up your two beaten slices of bread.
  3. Next slice all 13 olives into thirds and place on the peanut butter.
  4. Final step is to put the slices of bread togeather peanut butter and olive side facing out and enjoy a whimsical taste of childhood
